About

Welcome to my Website, this site features content from my trip to Japan in summer 2018. Japan has always been a country that I wanted to visit, due to their culture, rich history, and technological advances. I spent most of my time eating delicious foods and traveling to many different temples and shrines. Everyday felt like a new adventure from meeting someone new to seeing all the spectacular sights. Overall, my trip to Japan has been one of the most memorable experience in my life.
